My Favorites 2024

Best Movies
The following are some of the best pieces of cinema ever created:
Hunger Games (2012): Based off the novel written by Suzanne Collins, The Hunger Games is a brilliantly shot movie following a girl, Katniss, traversing a dangerous world set by her government, with one goal to protect her sister.
Catching Fire (2013): The second novel in the Hunger Games trilogy, Catching Fire written by Suzanne Collins, follows the Victor Tour of Katniss and Peeta, tensions rise in the districts. With the announcement of the Quarter quell the districts are told that the reaping will be done from the previous pool of victors, Katniss will be back in the arena.
Twilight (2008): Based off the novel by Stephanie Myers, Twilight follows an ordinary girl, Bella, who moves to a small town and ends up meeting a vampire, Edward, as their story unfolds its met with love and danger.
